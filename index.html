About Shifa Hub

Shifa Hub is a community-driven platform dedicated to making healthcare more accessible by connecting people who have excess medicines and medical equipment with those who need them.

Our mission is to reduce medical waste while ensuring that everyone has access to essential healthcare supplies.

We believe that healthcare is a fundamental right, and no one should struggle to afford medicines or medical equipment.

Our Mission

Reducing waste and promoting community sharing.

Our Values

Integrity and compassion guide every connection.

Our Community
